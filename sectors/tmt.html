2025 TMT Investment Themes

Based on Morgan Stanley, Goldman Sachs, and Deloitte research

Agentic AI Evolution

Autonomous gen AI agents increasing knowledge worker productivity. Enterprise software vendors delivering AI into all sectors, driving acquisition premiums for AI-enabled platforms.

Key Players: Microsoft, Google, Anthropic

NetCo Infrastructure Model

Asset-light telecom operators leasing infrastructure from specialized providers. Tower companies and fiber networks commanding 20-25x EBITDAaL multiples.

Key Players: Cellnex, American Tower, Crown Castle

Chiplet Architecture

Modular semiconductor design improving yields and reducing costs. Silicon photonics enabling next-generation AI data center infrastructure with premium valuations.

Key Players: TSMC, NVIDIA, AMD

Cybersecurity Consolidation

Cybersecurity startups raised $9.9B globally in H1 2025, 96% above 2024 levels. Enterprise breach costs averaging $4.88M driving demand for integrated security platforms.

Key Players: CrowdStrike, Palo Alto, SentinelOne

Edge Computing Deployment

5G and edge computing infrastructure driving enterprise private network demand. Hyperscaler capex exceeding $1B daily for AI-enabled data center capabilities.

Key Players: AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud

Supply Chain Resilience

Nearshoring and reshoring initiatives reducing Asian dependency. TMT companies investing in manufacturing diversification to mitigate tariff exposure exceeding $100B.

Key Regions: US, Mexico, India, Vietnam

TMT Valuation Framework

Investment banking perspectives on sector-specific metrics

Software & SaaS

  • Revenue Multiples: 8-15x for AI-integrated platforms
  • Key Metrics: ARR growth, NRR (120%+ premium)
  • Premium Factors: Proprietary datasets, vertical leadership
  • Growth Rate: 25-40% ARR growth commanding premium

Telecom Infrastructure

  • Tower Companies: 20-25x EBITDAaL
  • Fiber Networks: 8-12x EBITDA
  • Data Centers: 15-20x EBITDA (hyperscale premium)
  • 5G Assets: 30-50% premium for 5G-ready infrastructure

Semiconductors

  • EV/Sales: 3-6x (supply chain resilience premium)
  • Critical Factors: Foundry partnerships, advanced nodes
  • Risk Discount: 10-20% for China exposure
  • AI Chips: 50-100% premium for AI-focused designs

Media & Streaming

  • EV/Subscriber: $200-500 (ARPU dependent)
  • Content Assets: 3-5yr amortization periods
  • Sports Rights: Premium valuations for live content
  • Ad-Supported: 20-30% discount to subscription models
