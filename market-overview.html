Strategic valuations remain near historically low levels at 10.4x, creating opportunities for quality assets.

Market Drivers & 2025 Outlook

Key trends shaping global M&A activity according to leading investment banks

AI-Driven M&A Acceleration ðŸ”¥

Goldman Sachs reports AI-driven M&A expanding beyond technology sector. Focus on Infrastructure-Platform-Application framework driving dealmaking across industries.

Source: Goldman Sachs 2025 M&A Outlook
Scale Deal Prioritization

Bain & Company: Scale deals account for 59% of largest strategic transactions in 2024, marking shift from scope-focused M&A in previous years.

Source: Bain & Company Global M&A Report 2025
Private Equity Resurgence

Financial acquisitions surged 29% in 2024, with sponsors returning to market. Focus on distributions, liquidity solutions, and innovative structures like continuation funds (Bain & Company).

Source: Bain & Company Global M&A Report 2025
Geographic Rebalancing

Americas captured 14 of world's 20 largest deals in 2024. European M&A rebounded sharply after muted 2023, reaching normalized dealmaking rates (McKinsey, Goldman Sachs).

Source: McKinsey M&A Trends 2025, Goldman Sachs
Boutique Bank Performance

Centerview Partners ranked 7th globally for M&A in Q3 2024, highest since 2017. Elite boutiques gaining market share in large-cap transactions (LSEG Data).

Source: LSEG, eFinancial Careers Analysis
Technology Integration

Bain: 21% of M&A professionals currently use generative AI, with 50%+ expected adoption by 2027 for deal execution and synergy realization.

Source: Bain M&A Practitioner Survey 2025
